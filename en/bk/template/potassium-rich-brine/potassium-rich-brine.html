Potassium rich brine

The first considerable production will be based on the potassium-rich brine processing to produce fertilizers. In addition, sodium chloride (industrial and/or table salt) will be derived from this operation as a by-product.

  • LOCATION

    It is probable that the operation will be located in Concession 6 of the Bayovar project, Distance between Sechura and other brine operations

  • TRADE

    Our production of brine and other industrial products will be sold primarily in the local market.

  • DEVELOPMENT

    Our business will create jobs and new opportunities for the surrounding communities.

  • FASE DE OPERACIONES

    Las operaciones de potasio se encuentra en la fase pre operativa.

  • UBICACIÓN ESTRATÉGICA

    Las concesiones de Bayóvar están situadas cerca de infraestructura bien desarrollada, incluyendo el acceso a puertos marítimos de exportación y carreteras principales
