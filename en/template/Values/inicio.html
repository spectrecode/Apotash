VALUES

INTEGRITY

It is a company policy to promote high standards of integrity, through the management of business affairs in an honest and ethical manner. The company’s integrity depends on the honesty and sense of justice and integrity of each of the collaborators, which is reflected in the performance of their daily tasks.

RESPECT

Cultivate and promote respect for the ideas, customs and traditions of all the members of the organization and stakeholders interacting with Americas Potash.

EXCELLENCE

Apply responsible and serious practices managed by all members of the organization in order to achieve excellence.
