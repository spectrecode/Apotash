WHO WE ARE

Americas Potash S.A. (APPSA) is a Peruvian mining company, a subsidiary of the Canadian company, GrowMax Resources. Focused on exploration and production of phosphate and potassium-rich brines on concessions granted by the Peruvian State, located in the Bayovar project, Sechura Desert, Piura department.

The company's vision is to become a leading producer of phosphate and potash fertilizers in Peru.
