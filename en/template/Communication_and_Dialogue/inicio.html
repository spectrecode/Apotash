For a permanent dialogue with the populations in our area of influence, it is vital to have communication tools that transmit clear, transparent and timely information. For this reason, we issue the Boletín Comunidades (Community Newsletter) every two months, which is used to collect feedback, suggestions and requests from the community. We also have informative videos, radio spots and a mobile office, where the calls for employment and informative talks as well as feedback, requests and suggestions collected from different towns of the area of influence of the project can be made.
