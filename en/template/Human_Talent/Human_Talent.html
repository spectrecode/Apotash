HUMAN TALENT

Stephen Keith – President at Growmax Resources

Mr. Keith is a registered geological engineer and an accomplished senior executive with 20 years in the natural resources sector, with a specific focus on mining and finance. Mr. Keith has worked as a geological engineer, an investment banker and an executive for several public and private companies. Most recently, he held the position of Managing Director of Fertoz Ltd., a company focused on organic phosphate production in Canada and fertilizer distribution in Australia. Prior thereto, he was the President and Chief Executive Officer of Rio Verde Minerals, a fertilizer company focused on developing potash and phosphate assets in Brazil. Mr. Keith also previously served as Vice President Investment Banking at Thomas Weisel Partners, working on natural resources transactions in the mining and energy sectors. He has a Bachelor of Science in Applied Science with a major in Geological Engineering from Queens University in Canada, and a Master of Business Administration in International Business, with Latin America focus, from Schulich School of Business at York University in Canada. Mr. Keith is fluent in Spanish.

Hugo Inga

Hugo Inga is an executive with more than twelve years of experience in financial, commercial and business development areas among local and multinational companies. His experience covers sectors such as banking, energy, and biofuels. Since May 2017, he is the commercial director of Americas Potash Peru. Previously he was the Deputy General Manager of Baterias Alfa and the Commercial Manager of Maple Etanol. Hugo holds a degree in economics from the Universidad del Pacífico, a specialization in Corporate Finance from ESAN and is an MBA from ESADE Business School.

ABDEL (ABBY) BADWI

Abdel (Abby) Badwi is the general manager of Americas Potash Peru S.A. (APPSA) and was appointed as the Interim Chief Executive Officer and Executive Chairman of GrowMax Resources in November 2015. Mr. Badwi is an international natural resources executive and professional geologist with more than 40 years of experience in the exploration, development and production of natural resources in North America, South America, Europe, Asia and the Middle East. Mr. Badwi was previously Vice Chairman of the board of Bankers Petroleum Ltd., prior to which he was President and Chief Executive Officer of the same company. He is currently also a director for Arpetrol Inc. Previously, he was President and Chief Executive Officer of Rally Energy Corp., an oil and gas company with operations in Egypt, Pakistan and Canada, President and CEO of Geodyne Energy Inc., President and COO of Carmanah Resources Ltd., and Vice President International Exploration of Sceptre Resources Limited. Mr. Badwi is a geology graduate of the University of Alexandria, Egypt.

RAFAEL GRADOS

Rafael Grados is a business administrator with international experience in project management. Currently, Rafael is the Administration Manager of Americas Potash Peru S.A. (APPSA). Prior to this role, Rafael worked as a financial consultant for APPSA and as a management consultant for TIAA- CREF in Boston, USA. Rafael is a graduate of the Boston University School of Management with an approach to International Business. In addition, Rafael is a Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM - PMI).

CASANDRA LLOSA

Casandra Llosa has been the Social Responsibility Manager of Americas Potash Peru S.A. (APPSA) since August 2016. She has experience in the public and private sector. She worked in conflict resolutions as a commissioner of the Ombudsman’s Office Bureau for Conflict Prevention, and then led social responsibility programs in hydrocarbon and construction companies. Casandra is a sociologist graduated from the Pontificia Universidad Católica del Perú (Pontifical Catholic University of Peru) with a specialization in the management of local development projects and in the design and implementation of community relations plans with an intercultural and gender approach, always aligning social management with national and international standards, such as those of the IDB, IFC and the Equator Principles.

JAVIER WONG

Javier Wong is a Chartered Public Accountant with more than 20 years of experience in the extractive industry: mining and petroleum. He has held positions such as Controller, Chief Accountant and Head of Cost Planning and Control. He has been the Finance Manager of Americas Potash Peru S.A. (APPSA) since August 2016. Previously, he worked as a Controller at Gran Tierra Peru S.A., as the Head of Planning and Budget at Cepsa Peru S.A., as the Chief Accountant at Minera Teck Peru S.A. and held several positions in the accounting and finance areas at Pluspetrol Norte S.A. Javier is a graduate of the Universidad de Lima (University of Lima) with specializations in Corporate Finance, Taxation and International Accounting Standards (IFRS).

JOHN NICHOLSON

John Nicholson is a geologist with more than 30 years of experience in the exploration and development of natural resource projects in Latin America, North America, Europe and the South Pacific. John was previously involved in advanced-stage mining projects and related projects, holding management positions in many of them, especially in the project execution area. He currently works as a project operations manager. John holds a Bachelor of Science (Geology) from the University of British Columbia, and is a professional geologist registered with the British Columbia Association of Professional Engineers and Geoscientists.
