SALMUERA

The first considerable production will be based on the potassium-rich brine processing to produce fertilizers by using a solar evaporation pond technology. Additionally, sodium chloride (salt) will be derived from this operation as a by-product.

  • LOCATION

    The operation is likely to be located in concession 6 of the Bayovar project, 20 km away from Sechura.

  • TRADE

    Our production of brine and other industrial products will be sold in the local market.

  • DEVELOPMENT

    Our business will create jobs and new opportunities for the surrounding communities.

  • OPERATIONS PHASE

    Potassium operations are in the preoperative phase.

  • STRATEGIC LOCATION

    Bayovar concessions are located near a well-developed infrastructure, with access to export sea ports and main roads.
