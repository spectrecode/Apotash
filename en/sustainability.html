Social Responsability

Based on the company’s Social Responsibility policy, we contribute to the development of the communities located in the surroundings of our projects, seeking that the success of our business is reflected in the improvement of quality of life of the population closest to the project, so that our company and the communities continue to grow together.

This will be mainly achieved as follows:

1
BY CREATINGJOBS
+
+
AND STRENGTHENING PRODUCTION ACTIVITIES RELATED TO OUR BUSINESS.
2
BY IMPLEMENTING DEVELOPMENT PROJECTS
+
for the region to attain three of the Sustainable Development Goals

QUALITY EDUCATION
(O.D.S. 4)

HEALTH AND WELFARE
(O.D.S. 3)

SUSTAINABLE PRODUCTION
